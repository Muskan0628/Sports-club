Sports Club was conceived by a group of people headed by Chinubhai Chimanbhai; the then Mayor and leading businessman from Ahmedabad in 1965. The Sports Club gets its flamboyant and elegant design in the heart of city from the renowned architect Charles Correa
who also happens to be the designer of the soulful Gandhi Ashram. With a beautiful design and sprawling space; Sports Club has gradually spread its branches for other sports and has become an epitome of entertainment and leisure in the thriving city of Ahmedabad. Today, Sports Club is one of the most regal clubs of Ahmedabad and renowned for its state of the art amenities and splendid aura.Our objective is to make our students excellent in their respective field by providing the best facilities and modes to improve their skills to as high as they can. So, the question arises what are we going to provide them?
We are going to provide the most advanced equipment and modes. We are also providing them with professional trainers to guide their way towards success. We provide practice and training in our own built multi advanced Training Centre which includes an indoor stadium and an outdoor stadium. And we also take the students to the official grounds where the competitions are held for practice monthly. We are also having top players and earlier world record holders from different sports as our guest and mentor for the day. We organize a small interaction session with those players to clear your doubts and to get motivated by them.
